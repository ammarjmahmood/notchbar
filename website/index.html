Features

Everything lives in the notch.

NotchBar turns the dead space on your MacBook into a powerful, tabbed productivity hub.

📦

Drop any file

Drag files, images, or text directly into the notch. Everything is saved instantly and organized with timestamps.

📸

Auto-capture screenshots

NotchBar automatically detects new screenshots and pulls them into the Screenshots tab. No manual dragging needed.

📑

Tabbed interface

Switch between Files, Screenshots, and Settings tabs right inside the tray. Everything organized, nothing cluttered.

⚙️

Configurable settings

Change your storage folder, set an auto-clear timer, toggle launch at login, and more — all from the Settings tab.

⏱

Auto-clears

The tray tidies itself with a configurable timer. Default is 10 minutes. No clutter, no maintenance, no thinking about it.

How it works

Two ways to use your notch.

Hover and drop. That's the whole workflow.

1
👆

Hover the notch

Move your cursor to the notch area. The tray slides open automatically with a smooth animation.

2
📥

Drop files or take screenshots

Drag any file into the tray, or take a screenshot — it appears in its tab automatically.
